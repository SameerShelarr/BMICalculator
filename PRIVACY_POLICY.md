# Privacy Policy for BMI Calculator

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use the BMI Calculator app ("the App").

## 1. Data Collection
The App is designed with privacy in mind.
- **No Personal Identification:** We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- **No Internet Access:** The App does not require or use an internet connection. No data is transmitted from your device to any external servers.

## 2. Locally Stored Data
To provide its functionality, the App allows you to enter your height and weight to calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI).
- **Storage:** These values and your calculation history are stored **locally** on your device using a secure on-device database.
- **Control:** You have full control over this data. You can delete individual entries or clear your entire calculation history at any time within the App's settings.
- **Uninstalling:** All locally stored data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
We do not share, sell, or disclose any of your data to third parties, as we do not have access to it in the first place.

## 4. Security
We prioritize the security of your data. Since all information remains on your device, its security is protected by your device's own security features (such as screen locks and encryption).

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating this document within the App's source code or distribution.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the official repository or app store listing.
